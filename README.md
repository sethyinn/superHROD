# README: SuperHROD - AI-Powered OD Consultant

**SuperHROD** is an intelligent planning assistant designed to empower HR professionals with expert-level Organization Development (OD) consultancy. It bridges the gap between high-level organizational strategy and actionable project execution.

---

## üöÄ Overview

SuperHROD transforms how HR teams approach complex organizational challenges. By combining internal knowledge with industry best practices, it provides a structured framework for diagnosing, designing, and implementing OD initiatives.

### Key Domains

* **Organization Diagnosis & Design:** Structure adjustments, restructuring, and reporting optimization.
* **Culture & Engagement:** Transformation projects, leadership development, and engagement boosting.
* **Talent & Performance:** Compensation structures, performance systems, and talent reviews.
* **Change Management:** Process optimization and digital transformation transitions.

---

## üõ† Core Workflow

The assistant follows a professional four-stage consultancy lifecycle:

1. **Requirement Clarification:** Conversational probing to define objectives, scope, and stakeholders.
2. **Solution Design:** Generating proposals integrated with external benchmarks and internal data.
3. **Implementation Planning:** Breaking down strategies into executable tasks and timelines.
4. **PMO Support:** Automated generation of management artifacts (WBS, RACI, Risk Registers).
